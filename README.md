## What it does

- Fetches the **current season's anime** from [AniList](https://anilist.co) sorted by popularity
- Shows each title with its **cover, studio, score, episode count and next airing countdown**
- **Subscribe** to any anime â€” bookmarked locally on your device
- Sends a **notification** when a new episode airs for a subscribed title
- Checks for new episodes every **12 hours** in the background
- Follows your phone's **Material You** color theme
